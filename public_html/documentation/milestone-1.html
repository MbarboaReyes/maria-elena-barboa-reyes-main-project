My Little Hobby

My Purpose

I have always wanted to learn more about living off the land. I want my daughter to be able to have skills other than city life. The ability to have animals that help you live a life, also is very pleasing to me

My Audience

  • This is for all type of ages.
  • All types of backgrounds
  • This is a way of life you will make mistakes, but learn from.
  • You will need access to open space, depending on what you would like to grow.
  • Are you the type of person that wants animals?
  • Are you the type of person who just wants to grow plants?
  • Or do you want to do both? Doing both is going to depend on your location, laws and how much room you have.
  • You will need another source of income, depending on your hobby farm there is a good chance you will not be able to make it a full time job the first few years.

The Goal to all this

  • Learn more about the laws, pro's and cons of owning a small hobby farm
  • Live off grid as much as possible.
  • Learning new skills that were once a life or death skills.
  • Better understanding the respect humans and mother earth have.

This is what I want to accomplish with this website
